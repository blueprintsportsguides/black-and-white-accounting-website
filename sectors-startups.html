Startups

Startups face unique financial challenges, from choosing the right business structure and accessing R&D tax credits to managing early-stage cashflow and planning for growth. We provide specialist support for ambitious new businesses, helping you build strong financial foundations and make informed decisions as you scale.

Common Challenges

  • Business Structure

    Choosing between sole trader, partnership, or limited company structure has significant tax and legal implications that can impact your startup's future growth and investment potential.

  • R&D Tax Credits

    Understanding eligibility for R&D tax credits, preparing claims, and maximising reliefs can provide valuable cashflow support but requires specialist knowledge.

  • Early-Stage Cashflow

    Managing cashflow in the early stages, planning for investment rounds, and ensuring you have adequate working capital requires careful financial planning and forecasting.

  • Growth Planning

    Planning for scaling, understanding funding options, and making strategic financial decisions as you grow requires expert advice and forward-thinking support.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

Structure Advice

We help you choose the right business structure for your startup, considering tax efficiency, investment readiness, and future growth plans.

R&D Tax Credits

We help you identify R&D activities, prepare tax credit claims, and maximise reliefs to support your startup's cashflow and innovation.

Financial Planning

We provide cashflow forecasting, help you plan for investment rounds, and ensure you have the financial foundations to support growth.

Growth Support

We provide strategic financial advice as you scale, help with funding decisions, and support key business decisions with expert accounting insight.
