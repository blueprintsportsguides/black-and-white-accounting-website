Healthcare

Healthcare professionals and medical practices require specialist accounting support that understands NHS contracts, medical practice structures, and healthcare-specific compliance requirements. We provide expert support for GPs, consultants, dentists, and other healthcare professionals, helping you manage finances while focusing on patient care.

Common Challenges

  • NHS Contract Management

    Understanding NHS contract payments, managing contract variations, and ensuring accurate accounting for NHS income requires specialist knowledge of healthcare funding structures.

  • Practice Structure

    Managing partnership accounts, profit sharing, and ensuring accurate financial reporting across multiple partners in medical practices requires careful attention to detail.

  • Healthcare Compliance

    Navigating healthcare-specific compliance requirements, managing professional registration costs, and ensuring adequate professional indemnity coverage requires ongoing attention.

  • Private & NHS Income

    Managing both private and NHS income streams, tracking different payment cycles, and ensuring accurate allocation of expenses can be complex.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

NHS Contract Accounting

We help you manage NHS contract payments, track contract income, and ensure accurate accounting for all NHS funding streams.

Practice Accounts

We handle partnership accounting for medical practices, manage profit allocation, and ensure all partners have clear visibility of their financial position.

Healthcare Compliance

We help you navigate healthcare-specific compliance requirements, manage professional costs, and ensure you meet all financial reporting obligations.

Income Management

We help you track and manage both private and NHS income streams, ensuring accurate accounting and optimal tax efficiency.
