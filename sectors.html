Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about sector-specific support.

What if my sector isn't listed?

+

We work with businesses across many industries beyond those listed here. If you don't see your sector, get in touch - the chances are we've worked with businesses like yours before and can provide the support you need.

Do you actually specialise in my industry?

+

Yes - our team has hands-on experience across a wide range of sectors. We understand the specific tax reliefs, compliance requirements, and financial challenges that apply to each industry, so you get advice that's relevant, not generic.

Is sector-specific support more expensive?

+

No. Our pricing is based on the level of service you need, not the sector you're in. Specialist knowledge is part of what we offer - it doesn't cost extra.

Can you help with sector-specific tax reliefs?

+

Absolutely. From R&D tax credits for tech businesses to agricultural reliefs for farmers, we'll make sure you're claiming everything you're entitled to and not leaving money on the table.

Do I need to be local to use your services?

+

Not at all. While we have offices in Wraysbury and Herriard, we work with clients right across the UK. We're set up for remote working, phone calls, and video meetings - whatever suits you best.
