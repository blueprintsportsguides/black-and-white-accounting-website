Automotive, Engineering & Manufacturing

Automotive, engineering, and manufacturing businesses face unique accounting challenges, from capital allowances on machinery and equipment to R&D tax credits and complex VAT rules. We provide specialist support that understands manufacturing and engineering, helping businesses stay compliant while maximising available reliefs and optimising cashflow.

Common Challenges

  • Capital Allowances

    Understanding capital allowances on machinery, equipment, and plant, and maximising available reliefs requires specialist knowledge.

  • R&D Tax Credits

    Identifying qualifying R&D activities in engineering and manufacturing, and claiming enhanced tax relief can significantly reduce tax liability.

  • Stock Management

    Managing stock valuation, work in progress, and ensuring accurate cost accounting can be complex in manufacturing businesses.

  • VAT Complexity

    Different VAT rates for parts, labour, and services, plus complex rules around exports and imports, make VAT compliance challenging.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

Capital Allowances

We help identify all qualifying capital expenditure, maximise capital allowances, and ensure you're claiming all available reliefs on machinery and equipment.

R&D Tax Credits

We identify qualifying R&D activities in engineering and manufacturing, calculate enhanced deductions, and claim R&D tax credits.

Cost Accounting

We help with accurate cost accounting, stock valuation, and work in progress accounting to ensure your accounts reflect true profitability.

VAT Management

We navigate complex VAT rules for manufacturing, help with export/import VAT, and ensure compliance with all VAT obligations.
