What's on the Horizon This March

Year-end around the corner: For many limited companies, the financial year ends in March or April. That makes this month ideal for reviewing your accounts, planning for corporation tax, and dealing with any year-end adjustments before the rush.

Cashflow and budgets: With a new tax year starting soon, it is a good time to refresh budgets and cashflow forecasts so you know what the next 12 months might look like.

VAT and payroll: If you run quarterly VAT or have a March payroll year end, checking that everything reconciles now avoids surprises later in the year.

Last-minute tax planning: March is also a great time for last-minute planning. Review your position, tidy up your records, and make sure you’re ready for the year ahead.

R&D Tax Credits

Innovation deserves rewarding. We help you claim it properly. If your business is investing time, money and expertise into improving products, processes or systems - and doing so in a way that involves overcoming technical uncertainty - you may be carrying out qualifying Research & Development for tax purposes.

  • Quantify the claim properly
