Education & Training

Education and training businesses face unique accounting challenges, from managing course income and student fees to understanding VAT exemptions and claiming training-related expenses. We provide specialist support that understands the education sector, helping training businesses and educational institutions stay compliant while optimising their tax position.

Common Challenges

  • VAT Exemptions

    Understanding which education and training services are VAT-exempt, and managing mixed supplies of exempt and taxable services, can be complex.

  • Course Income

    Managing income from courses, student fees, and training programs, often with advance payments and refunds, requires careful accounting.

  • Expense Claims

    Understanding what training-related expenses are allowable, from course materials to training venue costs, requires specialist knowledge.

  • Regulatory Compliance

    Ensuring compliance with education sector regulations, managing student data, and meeting reporting requirements can be challenging.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

VAT Management

We help navigate education VAT exemptions, manage mixed supplies, and ensure compliance with complex VAT rules for training services.

Income Management

We help track course income, manage student fees, handle advance payments and refunds, and ensure accurate revenue recognition.

Expense Optimisation

We identify all allowable training expenses, from course materials to venue costs, helping you maximise tax relief.

Compliance Support

We ensure compliance with education sector regulations and help with any required reporting and record-keeping.
