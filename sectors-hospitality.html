Hospitality

Hospitality businesses—from restaurants and cafes to hotels and bars—face unique accounting challenges, from seasonal cashflow and hospitality VAT rules to managing payroll for variable hours and tips. We provide specialist support that understands the hospitality industry, helping you manage finances while delivering exceptional customer experiences.

Common Challenges

  • Seasonal Cashflow

    Managing cashflow through seasonal variations, planning for busy periods, and maintaining working capital during quieter months requires careful financial planning.

  • Hospitality VAT

    Understanding hospitality VAT rules, handling different VAT rates for food and drink, and managing VAT on tips and service charges requires specialist knowledge.

  • Variable Payroll

    Managing payroll for variable hours, handling tips and service charges, and ensuring compliance with hospitality employment regulations can be complex.

  • Cost Control

    Tracking food and beverage costs, managing supplier relationships, and understanding true profitability after accounting for waste and spoilage is essential but challenging.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

Cashflow Planning

We help you forecast seasonal cashflow, plan for peak trading periods, and manage working capital to keep your hospitality business running smoothly year-round.

Hospitality VAT

We navigate hospitality VAT complexities, help with VAT on food and drink, and ensure you're compliant with all hospitality-specific VAT rules.

Payroll Support

We help you manage variable payroll, handle tips and service charges correctly, and ensure compliance with hospitality employment regulations.

Cost Management

We help you track food and beverage costs, manage supplier relationships, and understand your true profitability after accounting for all hospitality expenses.
