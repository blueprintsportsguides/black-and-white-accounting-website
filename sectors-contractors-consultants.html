Contractors & Consultants

Contractors and consultants face unique challenges, from IR35 compliance and project-based accounting to managing expenses and optimising tax efficiency. We provide specialist support that understands the realities of contract work, helping you stay compliant while maximising your take-home pay.

Common Challenges

  • IR35 Compliance

    Determining employment status, understanding IR35 rules, and ensuring contracts are structured correctly can be complex and have significant tax implications.

  • Project-Based Income

    Irregular income patterns, multiple clients, and project-based work create cashflow challenges that need careful planning and management.

  • Expense Management

    Understanding what expenses are allowable, keeping proper records, and maximising tax relief requires specialist knowledge and attention to detail.

  • Tax Efficiency

    Choosing between limited company, umbrella, or sole trader structures, and optimising salary vs dividend extraction can significantly impact your take-home pay.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

IR35 Support

We help you understand IR35 status, review contracts, and ensure compliance to avoid unexpected tax bills and penalties.

Structure Advice

We advise on the most tax-efficient business structure for your circumstances, whether limited company, umbrella, or sole trader.

Expense Optimisation

We help you identify all allowable expenses, keep proper records, and maximise tax relief on travel, equipment, and professional costs.

Tax Planning

We provide strategic tax planning to optimise your salary and dividend mix, manage payments on account, and minimise your overall tax liability.
