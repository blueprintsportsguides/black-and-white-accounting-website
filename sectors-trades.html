Trades

Tradespeople and trade businesses face specific accounting challenges, from CIS compliance and tool/vehicle expenses to managing cashflow across multiple jobs. We provide specialist support that understands the realities of trade work, helping you stay compliant while maximising your tax efficiency.

Common Challenges

  • CIS Compliance

    Understanding Construction Industry Scheme requirements, managing CIS deductions, and ensuring correct tax treatment can be complex and time-consuming for tradespeople.

  • Expense Management

    Tracking tool purchases, vehicle expenses, and job-related costs while ensuring you're claiming all allowable expenses requires careful record-keeping.

  • Job Costing

    Understanding the true profitability of individual jobs, tracking materials and labour costs, and managing quotes versus actual costs is essential but challenging.

  • Cashflow & Invoicing

    Managing cashflow across multiple jobs, ensuring timely invoicing, and handling payment delays while maintaining working capital requires ongoing attention.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

CIS Support

We handle all aspects of CIS compliance, help with verification, process deductions, and ensure you're claiming all available CIS refunds and reliefs.

Expense Optimisation

We help you identify and claim all allowable expenses, from tools and vehicles to job-related costs, maximising your tax efficiency.

Job Costing

We help you track job profitability, understand true costs versus quotes, and make informed pricing decisions for future work.

Cashflow Management

We help you manage invoicing cycles, plan for payment delays, and maintain healthy cashflow to keep your trade business running smoothly.
