Freelancers & Creatives

Freelancers and creative professionals face unique accounting challenges, from managing irregular income and project-based work to claiming creative industry expenses and understanding tax obligations. We provide specialist support that understands the creative industries, helping freelancers stay compliant while focusing on their craft.

Common Challenges

  • Irregular Income

    Unpredictable income patterns, seasonal variations, and project-based work create cashflow challenges that need careful planning and management.

  • Expense Claims

    Understanding what creative expenses are allowable, from equipment and software to workspace costs, requires specialist knowledge.

  • Multiple Income Streams

    Managing income from various sources, including freelance work, royalties, and passive income, can complicate tax returns and record-keeping.

  • Tax Obligations

    Understanding when to register for VAT, managing payments on account, and ensuring compliance can be overwhelming for freelancers.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

Income Management

We help you track income from multiple sources, forecast cashflow, and plan for seasonal variations in your freelance work.

Expense Optimisation

We identify all allowable creative expenses, from equipment and software to workspace costs, helping you maximise tax relief.

Tax Compliance

We handle Self Assessment returns, manage VAT registration when needed, and ensure you meet all tax obligations on time.

Financial Planning

We provide cashflow forecasting and budgeting advice to help you manage irregular income and plan for quieter periods.
