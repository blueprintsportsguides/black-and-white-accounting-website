Our Services

At Black and White Accounting, we structure our services around three core pillars: Accounts, Tax, and Advisory. This approach ensures you receive comprehensive support that covers compliance, clarity, and strategic growth - all in one place.

Accounts Services Tax Services Advisory Services

Tax

Helping you stay compliant, efficient, and confident. Our tax services ensure you meet all obligations while maximising opportunities for tax efficiency.

What we cover:

  • Personal Tax – Self Assessment, tax planning, and support to keep your personal affairs compliant and efficient
  • Business Tax – Corporation Tax, VAT (standard, flat rate, partial exemption), Payroll & CIS
  • Property & Specialist Tax – Landlords, Capital Gains Tax, Inheritance Tax, residency & domicile advice

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services.

How is your pricing structured?

+

We offer fixed, transparent pricing so you always know what you're paying upfront. No hidden fees, no surprises. We'll agree a fee based on your needs before any work begins, and we'll let you know if anything changes.

Do I need all three services - Accounts, Tax, and Advisory?

+

Not necessarily. Many clients start with one or two services and add more as their business grows. We'll help you work out what you actually need so you're never paying for something you don't use.

Can I switch between services or add more later?

+

Absolutely. Your needs will change as your business evolves, and our service is designed to flex with you. Just let us know and we'll adjust your support accordingly.

What software do you work with?

+

We work with all major cloud accounting platforms including Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent, and Sage. If you're not sure which is right for you, we can advise on the best fit for your business.

How do I get started?

+

Just get in touch - by phone, email, or through our contact page. We'll have a friendly, no-obligation chat to understand your situation and recommend the right level of support.
