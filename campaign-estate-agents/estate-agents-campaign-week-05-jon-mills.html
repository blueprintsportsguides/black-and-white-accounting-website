A heads-up on something that may affect landlord clients who are thinking of moving their properties into a limited company. From April 2026, the rules on Incorporation Relief change. It will no longer apply automatically; it has to be claimed, and there are strict deadlines. Get it wrong and the client can end up with a large CGT bill that could have been deferred or avoided with the right advice.

If you have anyone who is considering incorporation, now is the time to point them to an accountant who can run through the options and the timing. We do this regularly and would be glad to help.
