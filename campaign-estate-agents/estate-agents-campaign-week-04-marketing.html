Rental Income, Expenses and Landlord Tax: Get It Right

Landlords must report all rental income and claim only allowable expenses. Mortgage interest relief for individuals is restricted to basic rate (20%), and the rules on what counts as a repair versus an improvement can be tricky. Many of your clients will benefit from an accountant who focuses on property.

We prepare rental accounts, include property in Self Assessment or Corporation Tax returns, and help landlords maximise legitimate claims: repairs, maintenance, finance costs (within the rules), insurance, and other allowable outgoings. We also advise on the £1,000 property allowance where it applies.

What we do for landlords

  • Rental accounts and income/expense reporting
  • Self Assessment and Corporation Tax for property
  • Expense claims and mortgage interest treatment
  • Ongoing record-keeping ready for MTD
