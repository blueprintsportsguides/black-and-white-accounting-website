Corporation Tax

Tax on the profits of limited companies. We handle calculations, returns, capital allowances, and ensure compliance with filing deadlines and payment requirements.

What is Corporation Tax?

Corporation Tax is a tax on the taxable profits of limited companies and other corporate entities. It's calculated on a company's profits (income minus allowable expenses and capital allowances) and must be paid to HMRC. The main rate for Corporation Tax is 25% for profits over £250,000, with a small profits rate of 19% for profits up to £50,000 (2026 rates).

Who Pays Corporation Tax?

All UK limited companies must pay Corporation Tax on their profits.

Limited Companies

All UK limited companies must pay Corporation Tax

Foreign Companies

Companies with UK permanent establishments

Clubs & Societies

Unincorporated associations with trading income

Co-operatives

Co-operative societies and community benefit societies

Tax Rates & Deadlines (2026)

Important information about Corporation Tax rates and filing requirements:

Main Rate 25%

For profits over £250,000

Small Profits Rate 19%

For profits up to £50,000

Filing Deadline 12 months

File return within 12 months of accounting period end

Payment Deadline 9 months

Pay tax within 9 months and 1 day of accounting period end
