Construction

The construction industry faces unique accounting challenges, from CIS compliance and subcontractor management to seasonal cashflow and complex VAT rules. We provide specialist support that understands the realities of construction work, helping contractors and construction businesses stay compliant while maximising profitability.

Common Challenges

  • CIS Compliance

    Managing Construction Industry Scheme deductions, verifying subcontractors, and ensuring correct tax treatment can be complex and time-consuming.

  • Cashflow Management

    Seasonal work patterns, payment delays, and upfront material costs create cashflow challenges that need careful planning and management.

  • Subcontractor Administration

    Handling multiple subcontractors, managing CIS deductions, and ensuring proper documentation requires specialist knowledge and attention to detail.

  • VAT Complexity

    Different VAT rates for materials, labour, and services, plus reverse charge rules, make VAT compliance particularly challenging in construction.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

CIS Management

We handle all aspects of CIS compliance, from verifying subcontractors and processing deductions to submitting monthly returns and managing refunds.

Cashflow Planning

We help you forecast cashflow, plan for seasonal variations, and manage payment cycles to keep your business running smoothly throughout the year.

Subcontractor Support

We provide clear guidance on CIS status, help with verification, and ensure all subcontractor payments are handled correctly and on time.

Construction VAT

We navigate the complexities of construction VAT, including reverse charge rules, different rates, and ensure you're claiming all available reliefs.
