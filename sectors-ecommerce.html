E-commerce

E-commerce businesses face unique accounting challenges, from managing VAT across multiple sales channels and international transactions to tracking inventory and handling high transaction volumes. We provide specialist support that understands the digital marketplace, helping online businesses stay compliant while scaling efficiently.

Common Challenges

  • Multi-Channel VAT

    Managing VAT across different platforms (Amazon, eBay, Shopify, etc.) with varying reporting requirements and different VAT thresholds can be complex and time-consuming.

  • International Tax

    Selling internationally introduces complexities around import/export VAT, customs duties, and understanding tax obligations in different countries.

  • High Transaction Volumes

    Processing hundreds or thousands of transactions daily requires robust systems and processes to ensure accurate record-keeping and reconciliation.

  • Inventory & Cashflow

    Balancing inventory investment with cashflow, managing seasonal demand, and understanding true profitability after platform fees requires careful financial management.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

E-commerce VAT

We help you navigate VAT registration thresholds, manage multi-channel VAT reporting, and ensure compliance across all your sales platforms.

International Tax Support

We provide guidance on international sales tax obligations, help with import/export VAT, and ensure you're compliant when selling overseas.

Transaction Management

We help you set up efficient systems for processing high transaction volumes, reconciling platform data, and maintaining accurate financial records.

E-commerce Accounts

We prepare clear accounts that account for platform fees, shipping costs, and inventory, giving you a true picture of your online business profitability.
