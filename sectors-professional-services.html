Professional Services

Professional services firms—from consultancies and law firms to architects and engineers—require accounting support that understands partnership structures, client billing cycles, and professional indemnity considerations. We provide specialist support tailored to service-based businesses, helping you manage finances while focusing on delivering exceptional client work.

Common Challenges

  • Partnership Accounting

    Managing partnership accounts, profit sharing, and ensuring accurate financial reporting across multiple partners requires specialist knowledge and careful attention to detail.

  • Client Billing & Cashflow

    Managing client billing cycles, tracking work in progress, and ensuring consistent cashflow can be challenging, especially with longer payment terms and project-based work.

  • Professional Indemnity

    Understanding the financial implications of professional indemnity insurance, managing risk, and ensuring adequate coverage requires ongoing attention and planning.

  • Time & Expense Tracking

    Accurately tracking billable hours, expenses, and ensuring proper allocation across clients and projects is essential for profitability but can be time-consuming.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

Partnership Accounts

We handle partnership accounting, profit allocation, and ensure all partners have clear visibility of their financial position and tax obligations.

Billing Support

We help you set up efficient billing systems, track work in progress, and manage client payment cycles to maintain healthy cashflow.

Financial Planning

We provide strategic financial planning that accounts for professional indemnity costs, helps you plan for growth, and supports key business decisions.

Time & Expense Management

We help you implement systems for tracking billable time and expenses, ensuring accurate client billing and better profitability insights.
