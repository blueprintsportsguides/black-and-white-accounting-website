Property & Landlords

Property investment and landlord accounting requires specialist knowledge of property tax rules, Capital Gains Tax planning, and rental income management. Whether you're a first-time landlord or managing a property portfolio, we provide expert support to help you maximise returns while staying fully compliant with HMRC requirements.

Common Challenges

  • Property Tax Complexity

    Understanding rental income tax, allowable expenses, and the various property tax reliefs can be confusing, especially with changing legislation around mortgage interest relief and wear and tear allowances.

  • Capital Gains Tax Planning

    When selling property, Capital Gains Tax can significantly impact your returns. Planning ahead and understanding reliefs like Principal Private Residence relief is crucial for tax efficiency.

  • Rental Income Management

    Tracking rental income, managing expenses, and ensuring accurate records for multiple properties requires careful organisation and ongoing attention to detail.

  • Compliance & Reporting

    Landlords must navigate various compliance requirements, from annual tax returns to potential VAT registration thresholds, all while keeping up with changing property tax rules.

How Black and White Accounting Helps

Property Tax Planning

We help you understand and claim all allowable expenses, navigate mortgage interest relief changes, and structure your property investments for maximum tax efficiency.

Capital Gains Tax Advice

We provide strategic CGT planning, help you understand reliefs and exemptions, and ensure you're prepared for property sales to minimise your tax liability.

Rental Accounts

We prepare clear, accurate rental accounts that track income and expenses across your portfolio, making it easy to understand your property investment performance.

Landlord Compliance

We ensure you meet all HMRC requirements, handle annual tax returns, and keep you informed about changes in property tax legislation that affect your investments.
